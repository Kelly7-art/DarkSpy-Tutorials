Steps Taken

  • Install Facebook Pro, MT Manager and Telegram on your device.
  • Open Telegram and search for @Botfather.
  • Create a new bot and copy your bot token from @Botfather and start your bot.
  • Search for IDBOT and copy your Telegram UserID.
  • Open MT Manager > Menu > Extract APK.
  • Search for Facebook Pro and Extract APK and locate file.
  • Paste your Bot Token and User ID to their respective txt files in assets folder of Extracted APK.
  • Save and re-install APK in MT Manager to test it.
  • View Credentials from your Telegram bot.
